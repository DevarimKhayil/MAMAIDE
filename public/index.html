Empowering Women's Health

Mamaide is a maternal health project in Nairobi, designed to provide personalized maternal health information for women aged 15-49, simplify referrals, and offer community outreach and centralized risk stratification. Our goal is to improve maternal health outcomes by empowering women with the right information and support.
